My Process

I

Research

I prefer to start at the beginning and ask you a few questions to get a sense of who you are and how we can tailor a plan to your goals and create a user-friendly experience. To design a personalised solution for you, I prefer to get a good knowledge of who you are and what you do.

II

Design

Think twice, design once. I collaborate with you to create a design that not only looks good but also works well. I'll spend as much time as necessary refining a notion until it's perfect.

III

Develop

All of my websites are built from the ground up, allowing me to provide my clients with totally customised solutions. I can have complete control over all areas of a project because I design and create it myself, so nothing gets lost in translation.
